# Project Aurora

Project Aurora is a renewable-energy initiative led by Helios Energy in Reykjavik.
The program director is Dr. Lena Marsh, who previously worked at the Nordic Grid
Authority. Aurora connects geothermal plants to a smart distribution grid.

## Partners

Helios Energy partnered with Vptr Robotics to automate turbine inspection. Vptr
Robotics is based in Oslo and builds inspection drones. The Nordic Grid Authority
provides regulatory oversight for Project Aurora.

## Timeline

The pilot launched in March and the first geothermal plant came online in Reykjavik.
Dr. Lena Marsh announced that the smart grid reduced distribution losses by 12 percent.
